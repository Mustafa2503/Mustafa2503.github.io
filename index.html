More About Me


Hey there! I'm Mustafa, a software engineering student at UoPeople.

I'm interested in building web apps, and learning topics relevant to environment and economics. I want to use my skills of coding to present solutions to solve some of the world's most challenging problems (climate change and wealth inequality).

I have previous experience both in Software as well as in Customer Support industry. For example, I've previously worked as a Trainee Software Engineer at MyBook Qatar (A subscription-based deals and discounts app providing offers for food, hospitality and lifestyle merchants in Doha, Qatar). Also, I am a Technical Support Specialist at Walmart in Ecommerce & Payments. Please see my resume (or continue scrolling down here) if you want to learn more about my experiences.

Above all, I am passionate about altruistic causes using technology. For instance, at UoPeople, I served as a pro-bono student ambassador, an activity to help raise funds for scholarship for the underprivileged people worldwide. I utilized the likes of Social Media and Digital Marketing to arrange mass donor campaigns for scholarship funds for the needy. Also, I provided counseling and mentored underrepresented individuals on the power of one-to-one connections via platform like LinkedIn and its efficiency in seeking career growth opportunities.

In my free time, I enjoy working out, listening to music, traveling, playing video games, and watching guinea pig videos =D
